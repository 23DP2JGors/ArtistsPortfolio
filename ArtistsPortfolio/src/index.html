Discover the mind of a true genius — Leonardo da Vinci.

A master of many arts

Leonardo da Vinci was not just a painter — he was a sculptor, architect, musician, scientist, mathematician, engineer, inventor, and writer. His masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper remain among the most famous paintings in history. Da Vinci's unique ability to blend art with science created works that continue to inspire millions around the world.

Inventor ahead of his time

Leonardo's notebooks reveal thousands of pages filled with sketches and ideas for inventions that were centuries ahead of their time. He designed flying machines, armored vehicles, submarines, and parachutes long before technology could make them real. His curious mind never stopped exploring, making him one of history's greatest visionaries and innovators.
